The Best Movies According to Achal

My top 3 Movies of all-time.

Sholay(1975)

Two friends are hired to capture the notorious dacoit Gabbar Singh. Famous for its dialogues, characters, and action-packed drama.

Dilwale Dulhania Le Jayenge (1995)

Raj and Simran fight family opposition to be together in this love story. A cultural icon starring Shah Rukh Khan and Kajol.

3 Idiots (2009)

Three friends navigate friendship, dreams, and a strict education system. Aamir Khan shines in this comedy-drama with a powerful social message.
